      3. Maintain Balance: Maintain an average daily balance in your enrolled Premium Savings Account that meets the Minimum Maintenance Balance of the Deposit Tier you have satisfied in Step 2. The average daily balance calculation will begin from Day 30 of account opening and will end 45-days following (“Maintenance Period”).

      4. Receive Bonus: After the requirements are satisfied, we will deposit the bonus within 30-days following the end of the Maintenance Period. The bonus will correspond to the Cash Credit Tier for which you qualify, based on the amount deposited during the Deposit Period and maintained during the Maintenance Period. If the Deposit Tier is higher than what is maintained over the Maintenance Period, or vice versa, you will qualify for the lower Cash Credit Tier. Please see below for an example.

      Example: Please see below for an example of how you can earn a cash bonus.  

      1. You open your Premium Savings account between 06/09/2025 and 09/30/2025.
      2. Within 30-days of account opening (the Deposit Period), you deposit $50,000 - $75,000 in net new funds.
      3. For the 45-days immediately following the Deposit Period (the Maintenance Period), you maintain an average daily balance of at least $50,000.
      4. The successful completion of Steps 1, 2 and 3 qualify you for a cash credit of $500. However, let’s say your average daily balance during the Maintenance Period drops between $25,000 - $49,999. In this case, you would only receive $250, notwithstanding your $50,000 deposit during the Deposit Period.

      The applicable cash credit tier will be determined following the close of the Maintenance Period and will be paid out within 30-days after the Maintenance Period (or 105-days following account opening). Cash credits will be paid to the account where the deposit is made.

This is not inclusive of money currently or previously held in a Morgan Stanley account.
