Options Regulatory Fee

A regulatory pass-through fee called the "Options Regulatory Fee" is a fee that national options exchanges assess their members in connection with options transactions. This fee varies by exchange and is based on a number of factors, including where an options trade is executed and whether the broker responsible for the trade is a member of a particular exchange.

Morgan Stanley Smith Barney calculates a blended rate based on the amount it is required to remit to the options exchanges. Effective for options orders settling on or after 7/1/2025, Morgan Stanley Smith Barney's blended rate is $0.0152 per option contract. This fee is rounded to the nearest penny. Please note the Options Regulatory Fee is subject to change.

This fee will appear on your trade confirmations as "OPT REG FEE," and will be reflected in both:
  • Your estimated totals on order preview screens
  • Totals for "commissions and fees" on transaction and order histories
