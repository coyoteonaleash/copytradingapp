    Deposit a check

    Choose from two convenient ways to deposit checks into your Morgan Stanley Private Bank or E*TRADE from Morgan Stanley brokerage account.

    Mobile check deposit

    Use the E*TRADE Mobile app to securely deposit checks directly into your account.2

    • Launch E*TRADE Mobile and go to Check Deposit
    • Enter the amount and select your account
    • Sign the back of the check and write "For electronic deposit" above your endorsement on all Checks deposited through the Service.
    • Snap a picture of the front and back of your endorsed check
    • Tap Submit to make your deposit

    Mail a check

    For checks made payable to E*TRADE or Morgan Stanley Private Bank:

    • Write your nine-digit or ten-digit account number on the memo line.
    • Sign the back of the check and write "for deposit only to [account number]" next to or directly under your signature.
    • Attach a deposit slip (if you have one)

    For checks made payable to you:

    • Sign the back of the check and write "for deposit only to [account number]" next to or directly under your signature.
    • Attach a deposit slip (if you have one)
    Note: The payee name must match the account owner name on the account in order for the deposit to be processed
