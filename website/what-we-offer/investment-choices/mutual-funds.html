    This multi-sector Fund provides broad exposure to fixed-income markets, offering a core holding that may complement traditional bond market investments.

    The Fund seeks to exploit investment opportunities in the high-yield market, primarily through diversified exposure to high-yield, corporate bonds.

    Looking both within and outside of the benchmark, the Fund seeks relative value opportunities across traditional investment-grade and high-yield bond sectors, also including nontraditional asset classes like non-U.S. sovereign and corporate debt, convertibles, and floating-rate loans.
